About the Workshop

This intensive 5-day workshop is specifically designed for Level 300 IT students to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry requirements. Participants will gain hands-on experience with state-of-the-art telecommunications equipment and learn from industry experts.

Workshop Details

Schedule

  • Day 1: Telecom Infrastructure
  • Day 2: Network Security
  • Day 3: Wireless Systems
  • Day 4: Data Center Operations
  • Day 5: Career Applications

Objectives

  • Understand core telecom principles
  • Gain practical equipment experience
  • Learn emerging technologies
  • Develop problem-solving skills
  • Network with professionals

Audience

  • Level 300 IT students
  • Basic networking knowledge
  • Telecom career interest
  • Hands-on experience seekers
